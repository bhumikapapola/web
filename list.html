What is Graphic Designing?

Graphic design is the process of creating visual content to communicate messages. It involves a creative and systematic plan to solve a problem or achieve certain objectives, with the use of images, symbols or even words. Designers work with a variety of communication tools in order to create visual solutions that inspire, inform, and transform. In order to effectively communicate their ideas, they need to have a strong understanding of typography, color, composition, and layout.
